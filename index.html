Cappucino

Cappuccino is a classic coffee drink made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam, resulting in a strong yet creamy and frothy concoction.

Expresso

Espresso is a concentrated coffee beverage brewed by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans, resulting in a strong, rich, and aromatic shot of coffee.

Lattee

A latte is a popular coffee drink made with a shot of espresso, steamed milk, and a small amount of milk foam, creating a smooth and creamy coffee with a mild espresso flavor.

Americano

An Americano is a diluted espresso, combining a shot of espresso with hot water to create a milder coffee with a similar strength to drip coffee
